About Us

Ayer Tena Police Station - Serving with Dedication and Honor

Our History

The Addis Ababa police force has evolved significantly throughout Ethiopia's history. From the early "Yeketema Zebegna" to today's modern Federal Police, Ayer Tena Police Station plays a vital role in maintaining peace, enforcing the law, and safeguarding the community with dedication and integrity.

Our Mission

To ensure peace, security, and the rule of law by building a professional police force that prevents crime, protects citizens, and upholds justice.

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's top five police institutions by 2022 E.C., known for professionalism, inclusiveness, and dedication to public service.

Our Core Values

Professionalism

Integrity

Respect for Diversity

Respect for Human Rights

Our Motto

"Protect With Courage; Serve With Compassion!"
